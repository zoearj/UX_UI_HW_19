Hello, I am Zoe, a freelance UX/UI designer. I thrive in leading stakeholder research and enjoy solving user problems that ultimately leading to improved user satisfaction, increased engagement, and better business outcomes.

My Skills

USER RESEARCH

Strategic user research is my priority, engaging stakeholders & conducting thorough user tests to inform a high-quality user experience. I know that diverse perspectives lead to optimal outcomes.

USER EMPATHY

As a designer in UX/UI bringing the needs, preferences, and emotions of your users is the heart of the role. This attribute is essential when it comes to creating accessible, inclusive, and immersive design.

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

Qualitative data informs my decision-making, uncovers patterns I would have overlooked, as well it identifies new opportunities that will lead to success for all stakeholders. When making design decisions testing with users can lead to analysis that is deepens my understanding of the solution.
